About Us

Welcome to our Movie Recommendation System for Indian regional movies! I am Mihir Mathur, a third-year engineering student with a passion for programming, machine learning, finance, and statistics. This project was born out of my love for movies and my desire to combine technology with entertainment. Understanding the challenge many people face in discovering great movies amidst the vast sea of options, I decided to create a recommendation system specifically tailored to Indian regional films. My goal is to enhance your movie-watching experience by providing personalized recommendations that cater to your unique tastes and preferences. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find your next favorite movie here!
